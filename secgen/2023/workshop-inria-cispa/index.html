Presentation of SecGen during the CISPA-Inria workshop

Members of the SecGen associate team participated at the kick-off event for cooperation between CISPA and Inria that took place at Campus Cyber (Paris) on November 6th. This event gathered about 20 researchers from both sides of the Rhine and should spark new collaborations between the two institutions.
